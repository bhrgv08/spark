1. Social security to live life with dignity, geriatric health care and safety & security and inclusion is critical. 2. Social pension be universalised. At least those at the ‘bottom of the pyramid’ as they say, women, disabled, oldest old should be universally covered. 3. National Programme for Health Care of Elderly (NPHCE) should be available in all the districts of the country and its budget should be available exclusively and also monitored likewise. 4. we ought to have at least one ministry exclusively addressing the concerns of the 140 million older persons who are soon expected to be 329 million.
Dream Sparks Foundation could consider leading a delegation to the Government along with the participants from some of the senior senior forums across the country as a collective voice of elderlies. Else the next best option could be to cover these aspects in the upcoming WEAAD reports / IDOP programs. Best wishes…
