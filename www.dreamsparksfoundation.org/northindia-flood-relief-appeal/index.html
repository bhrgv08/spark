Help the Flood Affected Victims of North India

Share On

North India has seen unprecedented flooding over the past few weeks. Settlements and neighbourhoods along the have faced the brunt of the flooding that has left thousands displaced. Livelihoods have been lost. Power and water supplies have been affected. The situation is grim and it may still get even worse. Dream Sparks Foundation is responding with relief and healthcare measures for the elderly and their communities affected by this natural disaster.

The Need

In the Rapid Needs Assessment undertaken by the Dream Sparks Foundation team in consultation with the Community Based Organisations and affected community, the immediate survival requirements have been identified as healthcare, food and essential commodities, temporary shelters and hygiene materials.

Healthcare

Dream Sparks Foundation is providing essential health services in the affected villages as well as relief camps, especially for elderly with Noncommunicable Diseases such as diabetes and hypertension, who are unable to get medical services. For epidemic prevention our teams are also distributing chlorine tablets.

The Relief Package

Dream Sparks Foundation is on the ground providing the following Daily Essentials Kits and Shelter Essentials Kits to the flood affected:

Daily Essentials Kit

Rs 3000 Per Family


Rice 5 Kgs (Basmati Rozana Rice); 10 Kgs Wheat Flour / Atta; 4 kgs Pulses (Chana 2 , Arhar 1, Mixed 1); 2 kgs Oil (mustard 1, refined one); 2 kgs Sugar; 1 kgs Salt; 1 Set Spice Powders (Chilly, Dhania, Jeera, Turmeric); 1 Set Bath Soaps (125 gms set of 5)

Shelter Essentials Kit

Rs 5000 Per Family


1 pc Tuffpaulin (Ribbed, 6 layered, 12x10 feet ); 2 pc Plastic mats Chatai 3x6; 1 box Chlorine Tablets (20 litres dose, 100 tablets); 2 Sets Plastic Bucket and mug; 1 Solar lantern Set; 1 Set of Cooking Utensils
